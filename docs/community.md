# Community

Nanotune is built by a small community-led team and we'd love your help. Whether you're interested in contributing code, documentation, or just being part of the conversation, there are several ways to get involved.

## Discord

Join our Discord server to connect with other users, ask questions, share ideas, and get help:

[Join our Discord server](https://discord.gg/ktPDV6rekE)

## Contributing

If you want to contribute to the code, read our detailed [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/Nano-Collective/nanotune/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) guide for information on development setup, coding standards, and how to submit your changes.

## GitHub

Head to our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Nano-Collective/nanotune) to:

- Open issues for bugs or feature requests
- Join discussions with others in the community
- Browse the source code

## Donate

Nanotune is built by volunteers and offered freely. If the project has been useful to you, a donation helps us keep it that way. Every contribution — however small — goes directly towards supporting the people who build and maintain Nanotune, and towards our goal of making quality AI tooling accessible to everyone.

We're currently finalising the details of our donation programme. Check back soon, or join us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/ktPDV6rekE) to be the first to know when it's live.

## Become a Sponsor

Sponsorship is another way to support the project and the community behind it. Sponsors help us dedicate more time to development, fund contributors fairly for their work, and keep Nanotune free and open for all.

We're not doing this to turn a profit — the Nano Collective exists to build AI tools for the community, and any funds raised go back into that mission: compensating contributors and making sure great AI tooling isn't locked behind a paywall.

Sponsorship details are coming soon. If you're interested in supporting the project as a sponsor, reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/ktPDV6rekE) or [GitHub](https://github.com/Nano-Collective/nanotune).
